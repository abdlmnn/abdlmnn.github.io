About

Hi, I'm Abdul — a programmer by trade, a photographer by passion.

What started as a hobby quickly became a way of seeing the world differently. Behind the lens, I found a new language — one of light, shadow, and fleeting moments.

I chase light across borders, from the golden deserts of Arabia to the tropical shores of the Philippines. Each frame tells a story of places I've been and the people I've met along the way.

This is my visual journal — a collection of moments frozen in time.
