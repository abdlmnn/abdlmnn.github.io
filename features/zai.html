Between Two Worlds

Born in Saudi Arabia in 2004, raised between cultures, and finding home in both places. This is my journey of childhood, culture, and belonging.

As a programmer, I see life as a series of code - each experience a function that shapes who I am. But unlike code, life doesn't always follow a strict logic.

"Home is not a place, but a feeling that exists in two countries at once."

Join me as I navigate the beautiful complexity of living between Saudi Arabia and Philippines.
